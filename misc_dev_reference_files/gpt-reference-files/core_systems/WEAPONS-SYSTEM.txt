### Weapons and Combat Actions

---

### 1. Axe

**Base Damage:** 1d12 (slashing)

- **Cleave:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d10 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Hits up to 2 adjacent enemies.
- **Rending Strike:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d8 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Target suffers a bleeding debuff, taking an additional 1d4 damage for 2 turns.
- **Axe Throw:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d12 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Ranged attack (20 feet). Must retrieve the axe next turn.

---

### 2. Bladed Fan

**Base Damage:** 1d6 (slashing)

- **Dancing Blades:** 
  - **Damage:** 3d4 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Single target attack.
- **Wind Cutter:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d6 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Ranged attack (15 feet). Pushes target 5 feet back.
- **Defensive Twirl:** 
  - **Effect:** Increases AC by 2 for the next turn.

---

### 3. Boomerang

**Base Damage:** 1d8 (bludgeoning)

  - **Damage:** 1d8 (bludgeoning)
  - **Effect:** Target is stunned for the next turn.
- **Returning Arc:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d8 (bludgeoning)
  - **Effect:** Ignores half cover.

---

### 4. Bone Spear

**Base Damage:** 1d10 (piercing)

- **Skeletal Pull:** 
  - **Damage:** 1d8 (piercing)
  - **Effect:** Pulls target 10 feet closer.

  - **Damage:** 1d6 (piercing)

### 5. Chain Dual Club

  - **Damage:** 1d10 (bludgeoning)

### 6. Claws

**Base Damage:** 1d6 (slashing)

  - **Damage:** 3d4 (slashing)
  - **Effect:** Single target attack.
- **Pounce and Rend:**

### 7. Compound Bow
